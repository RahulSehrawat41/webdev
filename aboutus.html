About Us

Our History

Started in 2019, RS CULINARY quickly established itself as a culinary icon par excellence in Gurgaon. With its unique brand of world fusion cuisine that can be found nowhere else, it enjoys patronage from the A-list clientele in Delhi-NCR. Featuring four of the best five-star Michelin chefs in India, you never know what will arrive on your plate the next time you visit us.

The restaurant was started by Mr.Rahul Sehrawat, in order to fullfill his dream of providing five-star taste at cheaper prices.Also the food which is left over is packed and provided to MCG so that no poor guy sleeps empty stomach.
